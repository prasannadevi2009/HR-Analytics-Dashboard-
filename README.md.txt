 HR Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview
Developed an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard using Power BI and DAX to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends.

Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

 KPIs
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate

 Analysis Performed
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Overtime
- Attrition by Job Satisfaction
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Work-Life Balance
- Attrition by Salary Group

 Key Insights
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Overtime employees showed higher attrition.
- Lower salary groups exhibited higher attrition.
- Employees aged 26–35 showed higher attrition.
